Colored note tile — the saturated green/purple cards from the dashboard "My notes" row.

```jsx
<NoteCard theme="green" title="Math conspect" body="A linear equation is…" date="May 05, 2025" />
<NoteCard theme="purple" title="Biology conspect" body="A cell is the basic unit…" date="Apr 29, 2025" />
```

Themes: `green` & `purple` (saturated fills), `greenSoft` & `purpleSoft` (tints).
